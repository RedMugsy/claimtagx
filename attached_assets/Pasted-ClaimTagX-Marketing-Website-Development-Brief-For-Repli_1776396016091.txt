1. Project Overview
Build the marketing website for ClaimTagX — a multi-tenant SaaS platform that replaces paper claim tickets with cryptographically signed digital tags. The site covers valet parking, dry cleaning/laundry, luggage/coat check, and repair/service verticals.
This is the marketing/landing site only. The actual web application lives at app.claimtagx.com (separate codebase, already built). This site lives at the root domain claimtagx.com and www.claimtagx.com.

7. Animations & Interactions
Keep animations subtle and performant — this is a B2B SaaS site, not a portfolio.

Scroll reveal: Sections fade-in + slide-up (20px translateY) as they enter viewport. Stagger children by 100ms. Use Intersection Observer or Framer Motion whileInView.
Nav: Background opacity increases from 85% to 95% on scroll past 50px.
Cards: Subtle translateY(-4px) on hover with 0.2s transition. Lime border-color transition on hover.
CTA buttons: translateY(-1px) + lime box-shadow glow on hover.
Hero badge: Pulsing green dot animation (opacity 1 → 0.4 → 1, 2s infinite).
Step numbers: Large, low-opacity (15%) mono numbers that add visual rhythm without competing with content.
NO: Parallax, particle effects, text typewriter effects, heavy scroll-jacking, or anything that delays content visibility.
